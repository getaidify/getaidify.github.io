Verify with Aidify

Aidify is a free typing analytics tool that allows teachers to evaluate students on their writing process, not just on their final document.

OUR PURPOSE

No more guesswork.
Measure student effort.

Through a simple Chrome extension, Aidify allows teachers to generate a report to evaluate student work habits, thinking process, collaboration and effort.

IMPROVE STUDENT
WORK HABITS

• Check student progress and time management

• See the time and duration that students are working on the assignment

SIMPLE, USER FRIENDLY

• Generates a graphical report with summary and stats

• Provides a video playback showing all student edits with timestamps

• No installation on student computer required ⓘ Extension is installed once on teacher's computer. Aidify can be used on any Google Docs shared with the teacher.

GROUP WORK STATS

• View individual contributions on group projects

• Easily see the effort each student put on the assignment

COMBAT AI PLAGIARISM

• Track revision count and history

• Identify text copied from an external source and pasted in the document

• Stop false accusations of AI plagiarism
